The Story

Why mbiX?

In every construction project, the same problem repeats: pricing data, material rates, and BOQ breakdowns live in Google Sheets — shared across teams, updated by dozens of people. But the real engineering work happens in Excel. Copy-pasting is error-prone, manual syncing wastes hours every week, and there is no validation layer in between.

mbiX was born from this daily frustration. Instead of waiting for an IT department to build a cloud solution, Muhammad built the tool where the work actually happens — directly inside Excel, with a full ETL pipeline, schema governance, and zero external dependencies. What started as a personal automation script grew into a complete Excel Intelligence Platform.
